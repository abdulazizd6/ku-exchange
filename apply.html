Applications for the 2025–26 academic year are open. Deadline: April 30, 2025.

Eligibility

Who Can Apply?

  • ✓ Students currently enrolled at Gyeongsan Jeil High School (Gyeongsan, Korea) or Chust Specialized School (Chust, Uzbekistan)
  • ✓ Grades 9–11 (second year students preferred but not required)
  • ✓ Basic English proficiency (program communication is in English)
  • ✓ Parental/guardian consent required for all participants
  • ✓ Able to commit to the full academic year (Sep 2025 – Jun 2026)
Selection Process

Submit Application

Fill in the form with a short personal statement and get teacher endorsement.

Short Interview

A casual 15-minute conversation with program coordinators at your school.

Results Announced

Up to 20 students selected per school. Results by May 15, 2025.

Orientation

Welcome session and pen pal matching in September 2025.

Questions?

Reach out to your school's program contact or use the form to ask questions instead of applying.
